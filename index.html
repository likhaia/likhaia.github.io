The Artist Behind the Craft

Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm the creative heart behind Likhaia. What started as a small hobby of making gifts for friends has grown into a passion for storytelling through handmade crafts.

Every sticker, polaroid, and craftbook that leaves my studio is made with the intention of preserving your most precious memories. I believe that in a digital world, something you can hold in your hands carries a special kind of magic.
