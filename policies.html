Studio Policies

Guidelines for a smooth creative journey.

Payment Guide

We require **Full Payment** before we begin production of your custom items.

Accepted Methods:

• GCash / Maya (Scan QR or Number)

• BPI Bank Transfer

Shipping & Handling

  • Production Time: 2-5 days depending on the item and volume.
  • Couriers: J&T Express (Nationwide) and Lalamove (Valenzuela/Metro Manila).
  • Packaging: Every order is bubble-wrapped and reinforced to ensure your memories arrive safely.

Returns & Refunds

  • Custom Items: Due to the personalized nature of our crafts, we **do not accept returns or exchanges** unless the item was damaged during shipping.
  • Proof of Damage: Please take an unboxing video. We will replace damaged items for free if a video proof is provided.

Content Usage

We may post photos of finished products on our social media. If your item is a sensitive gift and you'd like us to keep it private, please let us know during ordering!
